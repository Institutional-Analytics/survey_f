Affected Faculty Movement: Original Campus → Nearest Campus Now

Each card represents a campus and shows faculty who had to leave their homes due to the war. The horizontal bars indicate which campus is now geographically nearest to their current residence. This helps understand where affected faculty have moved and plan support accordingly.

Campus Redistribution — Resource Planning

This section shows how displacement has shifted faculty across campuses. Relocated Away = faculty originally at this campus who are now nearest to a different one. Relocated Nearby = faculty from other campuses who are now nearest to this one. Net Change = Relocated Nearby − Relocated Away. A positive value means the campus may need additional support and resources; a negative value means fewer faculty are currently nearby.
